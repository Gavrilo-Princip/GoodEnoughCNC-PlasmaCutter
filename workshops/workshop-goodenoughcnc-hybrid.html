Workshop: GoodEnoughCNC hybrid

An affordable and versatile CNC is just one workshop day away. On a day long workshop you'll build your own machine, learn about its operation and get it up and running. Our mentors will guide you step-by-step through the assembly process and will be available for all your questions.

Starting from a full kit of parts you will assemble the trolleys for the machine, drill the holes in main beams, assemble the machine frame then add the motors and belt drive and finally install the electronics. You will turn on your machine and test the motion in all three axis.

After the workshop you can add a hand-held plasma torch, a spindle for milling or some other tool to your machine and start fabricating parts with it. Note that the plasma cutter or mill are not included in the workshop.

Full kit of materials includes:

  • Pre-fabricated trolleys
  • Pre-cut main beams
  • Pre-fabricated plates
  • Essential Hardware (threaded and smooth rods, screws, nuts, washers, bolts, belts, bearings etc)
  • 3D printed parts
  • Pre-soldered and programmed electronics
  • Stepper Motors 1x Nema 17 and 3x Nema 23
  • Power supply
  • Other small parts
  • Electronics: ToslinkCNC fiber optic control system with Arduino GRBL controller

Machining toolhead is not included, but we recommend the following options that you may already have at home or can get before the workshop:

  • for mill: 0.8kW watercooled spindle + inverter
  • for plasma: Telwin Plasma 41

There will be demo machining tolls available at the workshop, such that you can test them and pick the right one for your work.

Find out more about GoodEnoughCNC hybrid here.

Workshop duration is estimated to 8 working hours with a lunch break. At the end your machine will be up and running and you'll be fully skilled to use it. Since it will be a long day of DIY fun, we'll also have some snacks and drinks ready to keep the energy up.

Tools and other equipment necessary to assemble the system will be available at the workshop.

What do you need to bring with you?

  • Laptop computer
  • 2x Metric 13 wrench
  • DIY spirit and motivation
