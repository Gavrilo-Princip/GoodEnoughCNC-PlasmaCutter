FAQ

What are GoodEnoughCNC Machines?

GoodEnoughCNC machines are a part of an open-source easy to built low price CNC toolset, by Institute IRNAS.

What are restrictions of GoodEnoughCNC Plasma?

With GoodEnoughCNC Plasma you can cut steel plates of a size 900x900mm and max 10mm thick (depends on plasma machine used).

Can I make one myself?

Yes, you will be able to 3D print the majority of parts yourself, others should be readily available on the market. Shortly we will be releasing the full system and make available KITs of all parts required to replicate it along with complete documentation.

What is the cost of building GoodEnoughCNC Plasma by miself?

Minimal configuration: app. 600EUR($670)*

Optimal configuration: app. 1200EUR($1350)*

*Depends which CNC controler and plasma is used. Price of the structural parts is app. 150EUR($170)

Is DIY kit avaliable?

We are working on that. It will be avaliable soon! Follow us...

Is it open source?

Yes, GoodEnoughCNC is an open-source open-hardware project. Read more about the license here.

Where can I get design files and source code?

You can check work in progress files for GoodEnoughCNC Plasma project in GoodEnoughCNC-PlasmaCutter GitHub repository. Full project documentation will be published soon. The documentation for other machines is being prepared and will be available soon
