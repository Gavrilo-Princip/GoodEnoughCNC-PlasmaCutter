GoodEnoughCNC Academy

Interested in attending? Subscribe and we'll get back to you when we have a detailed programme ready.

What is the idea?

Our week long GoodenoughCNC academy program will empower you to take open hardware out to the field and build fabrication capabilities anywhere around the globe. You will learn all the necessary skills to build, operate and maintain two GoodEnoughCNC machines, Hybrid and Troublemaker 3D printer. Thus you will be empowered to take these designs anywhere around the globe and replicate them there with a GoodenoughCNC Hybrid in a suitcase kit.

  1. You come to Maribor in October 2016.
  2. You learn how to build GoodnoughCNC hybrid and Troublemaker 3d printer.
  3. You learn how to operate GoodEnooughCNC machines.
  4. You learn everything you need to know about digital fabrication technology and processes.
  5. You meet makers from all around the world, share the experiences, learn from each other and have fun.
  6. You work on example projects on GoodEnoughCNC machines to learn about different use cases.
  7. You learn about applications of open hardware and digital fabrication in different fields (biology and other).
  8. You go home with one or two machines in a suitcase (Hybrid or 3D printer kit)
  9. You build your own machine when you get home and use it for your amazing projects!
