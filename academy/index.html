GOODENOUGHCNC Academy


5 days of workshops, 2 open hardware machines, 1 suitcase kit

When? 7-11/11/2016

Where? Maribor, Slovenia

Sign up!

What is the idea?

Our week long GoodEnoughCNC academy program will empower you to take open hardware out to the field and build fabrication capabilities anywhere around the globe.

You will learn all the necessary skills to build, operate and maintain two GoodEnoughCNC machines, Hybrid and Troublemaker 3D printer and go home with GoodEnoughCNC kit in a suitcase, fully equipped to make your own machine and start using it for your projects. As well, you will learn how to modify the machines to operate on solar power and replicate different open hardware projects.
